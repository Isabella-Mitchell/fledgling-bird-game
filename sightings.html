About eBird
  • eBird is an online database of bird observations. It was launched 2002 by the Cornell Lab of Ornithology at Cornell University.
  • eBird is among the world’s largest biodiversity-related science projects, with more than 100 million bird sightings contributed annually by eBirders around the world
  • This website uses the eBird API to retrieve data from the eBird website.
